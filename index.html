Select a Workout

Cardio Workout

Cardio workouts improve your heart health and endurance. Here's a simple cardio routine:

Jumping Jacks - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Benefit: Increases cardiovascular fitness and burns calories.

High Knees - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Benefit: Enhances cardiovascular endurance and engages core muscles.

Burpees - 3 sets of 15 repetitions

Benefit: Provides a full-body workout and boosts cardiovascular fitness.

Mountain Climbers - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Benefit: Improves core strength and agility while increasing heart rate.

Strength Training

Strength training builds muscle and increases strength. Try this strength training workout:

Push-ups - 3 sets of 12 repetitions

Benefit: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and triceps while engaging core muscles.

Squats - 3 sets of 15 repetitions

Benefit: Builds lower body strength and improves overall stability.

Lunges - 3 sets of 12 repetitions per leg

Benefit: Enhances leg strength and balance.

Dumbbell Rows - 3 sets of 12 repetitions

Benefit: Targets upper back and biceps, improving posture and muscle strength.

Flexibility Workout

Flexibility workouts enhance your range of motion. Try these flexibility exercises:

Hamstring Stretch - 3 sets of 30 seconds per leg

Benefit: Improves flexibility in the hamstrings and reduces muscle tension.

Quadriceps Stretch - 3 sets of 30 seconds per leg

Benefit: Enhances flexibility in the quadriceps and helps prevent injuries.

Shoulder Stretch - 3 sets of 30 seconds per arm

Benefit: Increases shoulder flexibility and reduces muscle stiffness.

Triceps Stretch - 3 sets of 30 seconds per arm

Benefit: Improves flexibility in the triceps and helps alleviate muscle tightness.
